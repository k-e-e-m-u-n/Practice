BEVERLY NAYA IS A FILMMAKER &
AN ADVOCATE She was born on April 17, 1989 in London,England to Nigerian parents. She has since become prominent figure in the Nigerian film industry.

Scroll down ↓

“After I graduated from university, I just knew that I wanted to act, and in London I could shoot a film probably once in a year and that's it. Whereas coming to this industry, I can build a brand as well as shoot films more often and be given a more diverse amount of scipts. So, I decided to come back for that reason.”

-Encomium Magazine.2011

Naya began acting at the age of 17 and studied film-making at Roehampton University, London. In 2011, Naya was named the “fastest rising actress” in the City People Entertainment Awards”.

Beverly Naya :)

- a multifaceted personality who has several interests. As an actress and filmmaker, she has produced a documentary film titled “Skin”, which explores the issues of colorism and body shaming in Africa

Naya is a fitness enthusiast who promotes the importance of maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle. She is passionate about advocating for social justice issues and has used her platform to raise awareness about issues such as gender equality, body positivity, and mental health.

She has been recognized for her work in promoting body positivity and colorism in Nigeria. In 2019, she was listed as one of the “100 Most Inspiring Women in Nigeria” by Leading Ladies Africa, and in 2020, she was named as one of the “Leading Lights” by ThisDay Style.
